Historical Importance

Posters like this were crucial in persuading the public to accept forced sterilization as a normal part of national health policy. Once such intervention seemed acceptable, it became easier for the regime to expand toward the euthanasia program and other forms of systematic murder. This poster demonstrates how harmful policies often begin not with violence, but with persuasive and carefully crafted messaging.
